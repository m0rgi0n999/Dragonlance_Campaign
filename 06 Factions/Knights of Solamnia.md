# Knights of Solamnia

> The most famous knightly order on Krynn.

---

# Overview

The Knights of Solamnia are devoted to honor, courage, and justice.

They follow the Oath and the Measure.

---

# Beliefs

## The Oath

"My Honor Is My Life."

## The Measure

A collection of laws and traditions governing knighthood.
