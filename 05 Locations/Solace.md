# Solace
> [!summary]
> A treetop town in Abanasinia known for its unique Valenwood structures and role in the early events of the War of the Lance.

---

# Overview

Solace is a major settlement in Abanasinia and serves as a crossroads for traders, travelers, adventurers, and refugees.

The town is built among enormous Valenwood trees whose branches support homes, shops, and walkways.

# Description

## First Impression

  

Warm lights glow among the trees.

  

Wooden walkways connect homes suspended high above the ground.

  

The smell of baking bread and spiced potatoes fills the air.
